Refer & Earn

Invite real friends, you both get $MOON — then earn more every time someone you invited goes VIP. Rewards compound as they upgrade.

VIP Points work for you

The VIP Points you earn from referrals aren't just a score — they climb your VIP tier, which raises your earn rate and your airdrop allocation cap (up to 5% at the top). They also prorate your own VIP upgrades: every VIP Point counts toward the next tier's threshold, so the more friends you bring who go VIP, the cheaper (or free) your own VIP climb becomes — you can reach high Depth Zone tiers without paying. Points are still always earned by playing; referrals never buy airdrop points.

Claiming

Your VIP-referral rewards stack up in the app's Invite tab — you'll see which of your referrals went VIP and a Claim button to collect your $MOON + VIP Points whenever you like.
