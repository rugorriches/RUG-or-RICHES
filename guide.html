About

RUG OR RICHES is a press-your-luck crypto tapper built as a Telegram Mini App. It turns the exact emotion of degen trading — the rush of a pump and the gut-punch of a rug — into a free, 10-second loop anyone can play with no wallet and no signup.

How it works

The core loop is simple to learn and brutal to master:

1 · Pump

Tap to stake your bet and push the live $MOON price up. The higher it climbs, the bigger your unbanked pot — and the hotter the rug risk.

2 · Read the heat

A rug-risk meter rises as you ride. Market events swing the odds live, and a "DUMP INCOMING" warning fires before a likely rug.

3 · Bank it

Cash out before the rug to bank your gains. Clutch high-risk exits pay a bonus; one tap too greedy and a hard rug wipes the pot.

4 · Climb

Banked $MOON levels your rank, funds upgrades and VIP, and a slice of every cash-out feeds your Airdrop Points.

Full walkthrough — A to Z

  1. Open the app. Launch from @RugorRichesBot or the website Play button. No wallet, no signup — your Telegram identity is your account, verified server-side.
  2. Get your starting stack. New players begin with a small $MOON balance and starting Airdrop Points so you can play right away.
  3. Set your bet. Pick a stake per round — bigger bets mean bigger pumps and bigger swings.
  4. Tap to pump. Each tap stakes your bet and pushes the live price up, building your in-round pot.
  5. Read the heat. The rug-risk meter climbs as you ride; market events (FOMO, bear raid, whale, volatility) shift the odds in real time.
  6. Manage the position. Sell half to lock partial profit, set an auto-sell target or a stop-loss, and watch for the dump warning.
  7. Cash out. Bank before it rugs. High-risk exits pay a bonus; ignore the warning and the unbanked pot is gone.
  8. Rank up. Lifetime banked $MOON levels you Shrimp → Megalodon, unlocking energy, daily-bonus and VIP perks.
  9. Manage energy. Tapping is free, but an energy tank gates long rushes; it regenerates over time, grows with rank, and can be refilled.
  10. Buy upgrades. Spend $MOON on eight permanent upgrades across Offense, Defense and Economy.
  11. Climb VIP. Earn or unlock four VIP tiers for earnings multipliers and rug protection.
  12. Clear quests. Daily, weekly, monthly and VIP quests pay $MOON and feed Airdrop Points.
  13. Claim daily rewards. Login streaks, a spin wheel, mystery boxes and a lucky draw keep the rewards flowing.
  14. Go social. Join or create a crew, chat, fight weekly Crew Wars, and invite friends for referral rewards (you both get 5,000 $MOON).
  15. Qualify for the airdrop. Sustained, legitimate play builds your points toward qualification, Elite status, and the cycle snapshot.
  16. Stack across six cycles. Each pre-launch cycle is a fresh season with a rising allocation — start early to stack the most.

Crews & Crew Wars

Crews are player teams. Join one or create your own, chat in-crew, and pool your banked $MOON onto a shared crew leaderboard. Each crew has a captain who manages the roster.

Crew Wars run weekly. Members earn war points for the crew through play and by rallying. Each week your crew is matched against a rival; pull ahead and unlock a Victory Chest whose reward scales with your lead and your personal contribution (claim from 5,000 war points). War points and the matchup reset every week.

Straight talk: crew leaderboards and member contributions are real and server-tracked. In the current build the weekly rival is simulated for pacing; live crew-vs-crew matchmaking is on the roadmap.
