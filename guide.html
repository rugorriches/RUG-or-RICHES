Learn the loop in under a minute

Tap to pump the chart, watch the heat, and bank before the rug. The deeper you go, the bigger the run — and the closer you get to rekt.

1 · Tap to pump

Tap to stake your simulated bet and push the price up. The higher it goes, the larger your round pot.

2 · Watch the heat

The rug-risk meter rises as you ride. Market events swing the risk live; cash out before a crash wipes your pot.

3 · Bank before rug

Click Bank to lock in round gains. Clutch high-risk exits pay bonuses, while a hard rug wipes the round's unbanked pot.

4 · Rank up

Banked in-game $MOON determines your permanent rank, unlocking VIP routes, energy capacities, and bet caps.

Game Systems

🌡️ Heat meter
The heat meter displays the current round risk. The higher the price pumps, the hotter the meter gets, signaling a higher chance of a crash.
🎭 Soft rugs vs hard rugs
A hard rug pull wipes out your entire unbanked pot for the round. A soft rug pull is a partial crash that preserves a portion of your unbanked earnings.
💰 Bank
Cashing out transfers your current unbanked round gains into your permanent spendable in-game $MOON balance.
✂️ Sell Half
Banks 50% of your round pot to secure partial gains while letting the remaining 50% continue riding the chart.
🤖 Auto-sell
Allows you to set a pre-defined multiplier target. If the chart reaches this level, the game automatically banks your pot.
🛡️ Stop-loss
Sets a floor multiplier to automatically cash out if the chart dips below it, helping manage risk.
🔥 Combos
Consecutive profitable rounds build multiplier streaks that grant bonus payouts on subsequent cashouts.
🔋 Energy
Each round consumes energy. Energy refuels automatically over time or can be boosted via upgrades.
🎁 Daily rewards
Includes streaks, mystery boxes, spins, and lucky draws that grant random in-game assets.
