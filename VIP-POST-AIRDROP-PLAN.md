# RUG or RICHES — VIP Value Plan (Pre- & Post-Airdrop)

Goal: make VIP genuinely worth buying **and** worth grinding for, without turning VIP into "pay for a bigger token allocation" in a way that reads as selling a security. Everything below is structured around one principle:

> **You never buy airdrop points or allocation. You buy/earn a higher *ceiling* and better tools; you still have to play to fill them.**

---

## 1. The allocation-cap model (what we just shipped)

Each account's maximum share of the airdrop pool is set by its **permanent VIP tier**, not by spend at snapshot time.

| Tier band | VIP tier | Max allocation cap |
|-----------|----------|--------------------|
| None | 0 | ~0.83% |
| Shallows–Reef | 1–5 | ~1.0% → ~2.0% |
| Twilight–Midnight | 6–11 | ~2.3% → ~3.9% |
| Abyss–Trench | 12–18 | ~4.1% → **5.00%** |

Points are **earned only by playing** (8% of profitable banks, quests, etc.). VIP raises (a) the **rate** you earn (existing `VIP_AIR_BOOST`, up to ~36×) and (b) the **ceiling** you can be allocated. Two players who grind the same points but sit at different VIP tiers get different *ceilings* — that's the VIP value, and it's tied to a permanent tier you can also **earn by playing** (see §4), not only buy.

**Why this is the most defensible shape we can take:** the cap follows the permanent tier (not the temporary Pass), the tier is reachable without paying, and spending mints zero points. It still must be reviewed by counsel before the snapshot, and the token should be US-geofenced until then.

---

## 2. Why become VIP EARLY vs LATE

**Early (pre-token):**
- Higher earn-rate compounds over the whole season — early VIPs reach the qualification gates and fill their cap far sooner.
- "Founder" status: early-VIP-only cosmetics + badge that can never be earned again (scarcity).
- Lower TON price to reach a tier now vs. later (we re-price up as the community grows).
- Bigger share of the *first* season pool, which is the largest and most-hyped.

**Late (post-token):**
- VIP still buys the better tools (bet caps, energy, earn-rate) and the cosmetic/access tier — useful for ongoing seasons, duels, and ranked.
- But the headline first-airdrop ceiling advantage is gone. Message: *"VIP is always good; VIP before the snapshot is best."*

## 3. Post-airdrop VIP perks (all compliance-safe)

These give VIP standalone value so it survives past the airdrop and doesn't depend on token speculation.

**Cosmetic / status**
- VIP-only chart skins, neon name colors, animated avatars, chat badges, leaderboard frames.
- Tier-named flexes (Trench III gets a unique animated crown).

**Gameplay**
- Higher bet caps & energy (already live).
- VIP-only **high-roller duel rooms** (bigger in-game $MOON stakes, VIP-only matchmaking).
- A **VIP tier of the Daily Challenge** with its own prize split.
- Better rug-insurance %, faster Auto-Trader.

**Access / community**
- VIP-only Telegram channel, early feature access, priority in events.
- A persistent VIP leaderboard.

**Economy**
- Better store pricing, bigger daily bonus, larger piggy-bank cap.

---

## 4. VIP points earnable by play (recommended next build)

Today VIP points come only from **referrals** and **TON purchases**. To keep the tier-cap model defensible (tier must be *earnable*, not only bought), add **daily/weekly quests that award VIP points**:
- Daily: e.g. "bank 5 runs" → +20 VP, "win a duel" → +30 VP.
- Weekly: "climb a ranked division" → +250 VP, "refer 1 friend" already covered.
- This lets a committed free player grind toward higher tiers (and a higher cap) over time. *(Status: planned — not yet implemented.)*

---

## 5. Founder Pass NFT (optional)

A one-time **Founder Pass** NFT granting **permanent top-band VIP + exclusive cosmetics**.
- Sell it as an **access + cosmetic pass**, never as an investment. No "buy this, the token moons" language anywhere — that flips it into a security.
- Hard cap supply for scarcity; reveal cosmetics only.
- Utility = permanent VIP tier + Founder badge + a cosmetic set. Keep utility purely in-game.

## 6. Compliance guardrails (non-negotiable before token launch)

- **No selling of points or allocation.** Spend buys ceiling + tools + cosmetics only.
- **Cap = permanent, earnable tier.** Pass (temporary) never raises the permanent cap.
- **Duels stay in-game $MOON.** Real-token wagering + house rake = gambling → separate licensing question.
- **Get crypto/securities counsel** (US Howey analysis) and **geofence the US** for any token snapshot/claim until cleared.
- **No guaranteed value language** for $MOON until reviewed.
