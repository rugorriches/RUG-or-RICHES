Most play-to-earn economies printed rewards faster than the game loop could absorb them. Modern GameFi design does the opposite — it caps proposed supply, releases it slowly, and builds gameplay sinks into the app so the economy remains stable. The proposed $MOON model is designed on these core sustainability rules.

1. A two-layer model

RUG OR RICHES separates off-chain gameplay tokens from proposed on-chain assets:

🎮 Game layer (off-chain, uncapped)

In-game $MOON balance and Season Points are earned and spent inside the game. They power upgrades, VIP and rank, reset and flow with seasons, and are never a tradeable asset. Unlimited grinding is restricted to this layer.

🪙 Token layer (on-chain, capped)

The proposed $MOON token is a fixed-supply, on-chain asset. The game layer only ever qualifies you for a bounded share of it via airdrop snapshots — it cannot mint new tokens. Scarcity is protected by design.

6. Liquidity & Launch

  • Deep initial liquidity: Seeded from the liquidity allocation at launch, with LP locks implemented to support stability.
  • Phased listings: Designed to expand DEX exposure as the gaming ecosystem matures.
  • Vesting calendars: Clear schedules will be published to keep circulating figures fully transparent.

7. Proposed Treasury & Governance

The proposed treasury is intended to support operations, and it will be vested over 48 months to prevent rapid drainage. Over time, community governance-lite may be introduced to allow players to vote on upcoming seasonal themes and feature releases.
