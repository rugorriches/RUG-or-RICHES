Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RUG OR RICHES ("we", "us", "the Game") collects, uses, and protects information when you play our free-to-play press-your-luck tapping game through the Telegram Mini App and related website. By using the Game you agree to the practices described here.

⚠️ SAFETY NOTICE: RUG OR RICHES is a game. In-game $MOON, Season Points, and Airdrop Points are gameplay features with no guaranteed monetary value and are not a financial product or investment. Nothing here is financial advice.

1. Who we are

RUG OR RICHES is an entertainment Mini App that runs inside Telegram. You do not need a crypto wallet, and we never ask for seed phrases, private keys, passwords, or recovery words. We will never request those — anyone who does is attempting to scam you.

2. Information we collect

Telegram account information

When you open the Game inside Telegram, Telegram provides us with limited profile data through its verified Mini App mechanism, which may include: your Telegram user ID, username, first name, profile photo, language code, and whether your account is Telegram Premium. We use the cryptographic signature Telegram provides to verify this data is genuine.

Gameplay data

To save your progress and run features like leaderboards and Season Points tracking, we store game state tied to your Telegram user ID, such as: banked $MOON, Season Points, taps, cash-outs, streaks, rank, upgrades, quests, daily rewards, owned cosmetic skins, crew membership, and referral relationships (who invited you and who you invited).

Payments (Telegram Stars)

We may store Telegram Stars payment references, including Telegram payment charge IDs where applicable, to provide purchase history, support, refund handling, fraud prevention, and account reconciliation. Telegram Stars are handled through Telegram’s payment system; RUG OR RICHES never asks for wallet seed phrases, private keys, or recovery words.

Communications (crew chat)

If you join a crew and use crew chat, the messages you send, your display name, and timestamps are stored so other crew members can see them. Do not share sensitive personal information in chat.

Notifications

We may send you in-app and Telegram bot messages (for example, re-engagement reminders, event alerts, or a weekly recap). These come from the official bot. You can stop them at any time (see "Your choices").

Technical & local data

  • Local storage: the Game saves a copy of your progress in your device/browser local storage so it loads quickly and works offline. This stays on your device.
  • Usage & security data: our hosting and database providers may automatically process technical information such as IP address, device/browser type, and request logs to operate the service, prevent abuse, and detect cheating.

3. How we use your information

  • Run the Game, save your progress, and sync it across sessions.
  • Operate leaderboards, ranks, crews, referrals, and Season Points tracking.
  • Process and credit Telegram Stars purchases.
  • Send game-related notifications you can opt out of.
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate cheating, fraud, and abuse.
  • Maintain, debug, and improve the Game.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help us run the Game, and only as needed:

  • Telegram — the platform the Game runs on, identity verification, messaging, and Stars payments. See Telegram's own privacy policy.
  • Hosting & database providers (e.g., our serverless hosting and managed database) — to store game state and serve the app.
  • Other players — your public display name, rank, crew, and leaderboard standing are visible to others by design. Your Telegram phone number and contact details are never shown.
  • Legal — if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of our players or us.

5. Data retention

We keep your game data while your account is active so your progress persists. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see below). Some records (for example, payment/transaction logs) may be retained where required for legitimate or legal reasons.

6. Your rights & choices

  • Access / deletion: you can request a copy of, or the deletion of, the data tied to your Telegram ID by contacting us (Section 9). Deleting your data erases your in-game progress.
  • Notifications: you can mute or block the bot in Telegram to stop messages.
  • Local data: clearing your browser/Telegram cache or resetting progress in-game removes the local copy on your device.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights (such as under the GDPR or CCPA), including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data, and to lodge a complaint with a regulator.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including Telegram's signed-request verification and access controls on our database. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

The Game is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will remove it.
