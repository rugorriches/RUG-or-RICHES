Basics

Gameplay

How do I play?
Set your simulated bet, tap to pump the chart, watch the heat meter climb, and click 'Bank' to lock in your gains before the chart rugs. It is a game of quick reflexes and nerve.
What does “rug” mean in the game?
A 'rug' or 'rug pull' refers to the simulated market crash. If the chart rugs before you cash out, your unbanked position for that round is wiped out.
What is the heat meter?
The heat meter shows the current risk level of the round. The higher the price goes, the hotter it gets, and the closer you are to a potential rug pull.
What is a soft rug?
A soft rug is a partial crash where only a portion of your unbanked round pot is lost, allowing you to salvage the remaining amount.
What is a hard rug?
A hard rug is a total crash. It wipes out 100% of your unbanked round gains, leaving you rekt for that round.
What does Bank do?
Clicking Bank cashes out your current round gains, transferring them permanently into your permanent, spendable in-game $MOON balance.
What does Sell Half do?
Sell Half cashes out 50% of your current round pot, securing partial gains while letting the other half continue to ride the chart.
What is auto-sell?
Auto-sell is a feature where you set a target multiplier (e.g. 2.0x). If the chart pumps to this level, the game automatically banks your pot.
What is stop-loss?
Stop-loss lets you define a safety floor multiplier. If the chart dips below this level, the game automatically banks your pot to preserve points.
How does energy work?
Active taps consume energy. Your energy tank slowly regenerates over time, and you can increase its capacity and speed via permanent upgrades.
How do ranks work?
Ranks represent your lifetime banked in-game $MOON. Ranks climb from Shrimp up to Megalodon, unlocking higher bet ceilings and energy perks. Check the full chart on the Guide page.
How does VIP work?
VIP tiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond) offer in-game benefits like earnings multipliers, bet cap boosts, and rug shields. They represent in-game upgrades only.
Are paid boosts required to win?
No. All upgrades and features can be unlocked over time using in-game $MOON earned from playing. Paid items are optional convenience tools.

Tokenomics

What is the planned supply?
The proposed model establishes a fixed maximum supply of 1,000,000,000 (1 Billion) tokens. No new tokens can ever be minted once deployed.
Is there a private sale or VC allocation?
No. The proposed token distribution includes 0% for private investors or venture capital, prioritizing public players and community growth. For details, see the Tokenomics page.
What does fixed supply mean?
Fixed supply means that the total number of tokens is permanently capped at launch, protecting the ecosystem from supply inflation.
What is the two-layer economy?
It is a system that separates unlimited in-game points (for bet limits, upgrades) from the capped, proposed on-chain token. This prevents gameplay loop inflation from diluting token scarcity.
What are proposed sinks?
Sinks are mechanisms that consume or lock up tokens (such as entry fees for competitive matches, cosmetic skins marketplace, and staking).
What is buyback/burn?
A proposed framework where a portion of game revenue could be used to purchase tokens from the market and permanently burn them to support ecosystem sustainability.

Fairness & Security

Is the game server-authoritative?
Yes. Every tap, bet, cash-out, and reward claim is computed and saved directly on our secure backend database, preventing client-side hacking.
Can users cheat?
Our servers enforce strict verification limits (e.g. max tap frequency, bet caps, request verification). Suspicious or automated accounts are flagged and excluded.
What is provably fair?
Provably fair is a cryptographic protocol that lets players verify that each round's rug crash multiplier was pre-determined and not altered during play.
Is commit-reveal live?
Yes — the Daily Crash pick uses provably-fair commit-reveal today: the day's seed hash is published before any picks are made, and the raw seed is revealed the next day so anyone can verify the crash point. Note that the main tapper rounds are a fast client-side simulation (for feel and pacing), not a provably-fair market — the commit-reveal fairness applies to the Daily Crash.
Do I ever need to share a seed phrase?
No. RUG OR RICHES will never ask for your seed phrase, private keys, passwords, or recovery words. If anyone does, block and report them immediately.
What data does the app collect?
We collect basic Telegram profile info (user ID, username), game state data, and Telegram Stars payment references for verification. Read our Privacy Policy.
