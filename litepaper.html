1. Executive Summary

RUG OR RICHES is a press-your-luck game designed as a Telegram Mini App. It distills the psychological adrenaline of degen trading into a fast, engaging, simulated loop. The ecosystem operates on a sustainable dual-layer system separating off-chain gameplay points from proposed on-chain scarcity.

3. Game Loop

The core game loop consists of tap-to-bet mechanics, real-time risk escalation via the heat meter, and strategic cash-outs. Detailed explanations of auto-sell, stop-loss, combos, and permanent stat upgrades are documented in the Game Guide.

4. Economy Design

The economy utilizes a dual-layer structure to protect scarcity:

  • Gameplay Layer: Off-chain, uncapped in-game $MOON points used for progression, upgrades, and ranks (Shrimp to Megalodon).
  • Proposed Token Layer: Hard-capped on-chain token model ($MOON token) that cannot be inflated by active grinding.

5. Season Points & Proposed Airdrop

Season Points measure seasonal engagement. Points are off-chain and carry no monetary value or conversion guarantees. If a future token distribution is approved, snapshot cycles (Genesis to Launch) may consider Season Points and gameplay logs, subject to eligibility audits. For details, see the Airdrop Page.

6. Proposed Tokenomics Overview

The proposed token model has a fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 $MOON tokens. Roughly 65% is allocated for community-led systems (airdrops, seasons, referral bonuses), with team and treasury allocations subject to multi-year linear vesting cliffs. The full allocation chart is available on the Tokenomics Page.

7. Sinks & Slices

Long-term sustainability relies on balancing token sources (limited emissions) with sinks (cosmetic marketplaces, tournament fees, staking, and optional buyback/burn programs funded by Stars revenue). These mechanisms are proposed and subject to legal, tax, and governance reviews.

8. Fairness & Anti-Cheat

The RUG OR RICHES economy is strictly server-authoritative. Taps, bets, point syncs, duel settlements, and ranked results are validated on our secure backend, rendering client-side memory editors useless. Identity verification uses cryptographically signed Telegram hashes. Automated accounts, bots, and Sybil behaviors are automatically flagged and excluded from leaderboards and proposed snapshots.

9. Roadmap

  • Phase 1 — Foundation (Live): Telegram game loop, permanent upgrades, VIP tiers & the rank ladder, the energy economy, and Season Points tracking.
  • Phase 2 — Competitive layer (Live): Server-authoritative economy, anti-cheat, provably-fair Daily Crash (commit-reveal), async same-seed duels, an Elo ranked ladder, all-crews weekly Crew Wars, live boards, and peer gifting.
  • Phase 3 — Real-time & depth (Next): Real-time PvP on a shared live chart, synchronized communal rounds, scheduled tournaments, spectate/replays, and deeper crew tooling.
  • Phase 4 — Pre-launch seasons (Proposed): The six pre-launch airdrop snapshot cycles, qualification audits, and Sybil/anti-bot filtering ahead of any token event.
  • Phase 5 — Compliance & token (Proposed): Legal, tax & jurisdictional review, smart-contract audit, TGE on TON, deep locked liquidity, and the Stars-funded buyback-and-burn program.
  • Phase 6 — Ecosystem & growth (Proposed): Phased DEX/CEX listings, staking & holder perks, governance-lite, creator/influencer & project partnerships, and ongoing user acquisition to grow the active player base that funds the whole loop.

Sequencing is intentional: game depth and a real, engaged player base come before any token event, so the economy is funded by genuine activity rather than speculation. Timelines are deliberately not fixed; token-related phases are contingent on legal review and are not promises.

10. Risks & Disclaimers

Participation in the RUG OR RICHES ecosystem carries the following risk factors:

  • Beta Product Status: The game is in a beta stage and may contain bugs, balance discrepancies, or technical service interruptions.
  • No Token Guarantees: No token launch is guaranteed, and in-game points/Season Points have no cash value.
  • Regulatory Changes: International laws regarding digital items and token claims are subject to change. Players in restricted jurisdictions may be excluded from claims.
  • Eligibility Audits: Exploit attempts, botting, or multiple account setups will void all points and allocations. All token claim participants must be 18+.
  • Optional Purchases: Purchases of Telegram Stars or VIP perks are strictly optional and do not guarantee odds, prizes, or token eligibility.
