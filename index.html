Good to know

FAQ

Is it free? Do I need a wallet?
Yes, totally free, and no wallet needed to play. Just open it and tap. A wallet only becomes relevant if/when the $MOON token launches.
What are Airdrop Points?
Points are your share of the future $MOON token pool. You earn them by banking $MOON, keeping streaks, and clearing daily & social quests. The more points, the bigger your allocation at season end.
What does "rug" mean here?
When you tap, the price pumps but rug-risk builds. If it rugs before you sell, your unbanked position is wiped (a soft rug returns part of it). It's self-aware crypto humour — time your exit and you get rich; get greedy and you get rekt.
How do I earn more?
Cash out at high risk for bonuses, build win streaks, buy upgrades & VIP, complete quests, and invite friends (you both get 5,000 $MOON). Crews let you climb team leaderboards together.
Is $MOON a real cryptocurrency?
Not yet — today it's off-chain in-game points. A tradable token would only launch after legal review. Treat allocations as in-game until any official token event is announced.
