Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains the small amounts of data that RUG OR RICHES stores on your device when you use the Game and website. We keep this minimal — we do not use advertising cookies or sell your data.

1. Local storage (how we save your game)

The Game uses your browser/device local storage to save a copy of your progress (balances, settings, preferences, and gameplay UI state) so the Game loads instantly and works offline. This storage is essential for the Game to function and is stored locally on your device. No seed phrases, private keys, or wallet recovery phrases are ever stored.

2. Essential / functional cookies

Our hosting and platform providers (such as our website host and Telegram) may set a small number of strictly necessary cookies or session identifiers to deliver the page, keep it secure, handle Telegram app state, and prevent abuse. These are required for the service to work.

3. What we do NOT use

  • No advertising or marketing cookies.
  • No third-party ad-network or cross-site tracking.
  • No selling of your data.

4. Managing or clearing storage

You can clear cookies and local storage at any time through your browser settings or by clearing the Telegram app cache. You can also reset your in-game progress from within the Game's settings. Clearing this data will remove your locally saved progress on that device (cloud-synced progress tied to your Telegram account may still be restored when you reconnect).

5. More information

For how we handle your information overall, see our Privacy Policy. Questions? Reach us via the official Telegram bot support flow or the official community links listed on this site.
