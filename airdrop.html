1. Season 1 Overview

During Season 1, players can participate in the free gameplay loop and quests to accumulate Season Points (referred to in some app interfaces as Airdrop Points). These points measure gameplay engagement and community participation. No purchase or wallet connection is required to play or earn Season Points.

2. How Season Points Work

Season Points are off-chain, virtual indicators of active play. They have no cash value and do not represent a financial claim. If a future token distribution is approved, Season Points may be considered as part of eligibility evaluations, subject to review. Legitimate gameplay history, anti-cheat reviews, platform compliance, and jurisdiction restrictions will apply.

3. What Points Do NOT Mean

  • Season Points do not represent ownership, equity, or debt in any entity.
  • Season Points do not guarantee any distribution, claim, or allocation of on-chain tokens.
  • Paid items, VIP upgrades, Stars purchases, or Season Pass perks do not guarantee token eligibility, token allocation, prize odds, or monetary value. Any paid perks are strictly for in-game progression or entertainment.

5. Ways to Earn Points

  • Gameplay cash-outs: Banking profitable in-game rounds automatically adds to your Season Points.
  • Daily & social quests: Complete in-game tasks and achievements. (Note: social posts and referrals are completely optional gameplay actions and do not guarantee allocations).
  • Streaks & rewards: Claim daily streaks and spin wheel items.
  • Referrals & crews: Invite friends — you both receive in-game $MOON, and the inviter earns bonus in-game Season Points. When someone you invited activates a VIP membership, you also earn in-game VIP Points and bonus $MOON. All referral rewards are optional in-game items only and never guarantee any token allocation, eligibility, or value. Referral disclosure rule: if you share RUG OR RICHES because you may receive in-game rewards, disclose that connection clearly (e.g. "#ad" or "#sponsored").
  • VIP memberships (Depth Zones): An active VIP membership applies an in-game multiplier that greatly increases how quickly you earn Season Points from gameplay, on top of higher earnings, bet caps and energy. VIP is optional and purchased with Telegram Stars or TON; it does not guarantee token eligibility, allocation, prizes, odds, or monetary value.
  • Season Pass: Adds +50,000 Season Points in-game bonus for progression/entertainment only. *Note: Season Pass perks are optional in-game entertainment benefits. Any Season Points associated with a Season Pass do not guarantee token eligibility, token allocation, prizes, odds, rewards, or monetary value. This mechanic should be reviewed by legal counsel before any future token distribution terms are finalized.

5a. VIP Memberships — “Depth Zones”

VIP is an optional, paid membership with 18 levels across six tiers (Shallows → Reef → Twilight → Midnight → Abyss → Trench), purchasable with Telegram Stars or TON. Each level multiplies the Season Points you earn by playing (banking, quests, dailies) — VIP does not grant Season Points for spending; it only accelerates what you earn through gameplay. It also raises $MOON earnings, bet cap and energy; higher tiers add rug protection and whale perks. You climb VIP tiers with VIP Points earned from VIP-point quests, referrals, or TON / Stars purchases (a loyalty score for in-game items). VIP perks are entertainment benefits and do not guarantee any token eligibility, allocation, odds, prizes, or monetary value.

Fair distribution. If any future distribution is ever approved, per-player allocation is weighted toward sustained play and capped per account by VIP tier — from about 0.30% of a cycle's pool with no VIP, up to a maximum of 5% at the top tier — so a dedicated player can earn a meaningful share, but no single account can dominate a cycle. VIP raises your ceiling and earn rate; it never buys points or a guaranteed allocation, and every point is still earned through play.

Community-funded — no VCs, no private sale. RUG OR RICHES is independent. Revenue from VIP, cosmetics and Telegram Stars / TON goes directly into building and operating the game. There is no venture-capital backing and no private token sale.

6. Anti-Cheat & Eligibility

To preserve fairness, all gameplay records are audited server-side. Cheating, bot usage, scripts, multi-accounting, or Sybil attacks will result in immediate disqualification, account bans, and forfeiture of all points and balances.

If a future distribution is approved, eligibility targets may be considered to reward consistent play (e.g. active play days, taps, cashouts), but these targets are illustrative, subject to change, and do not create a binding entitlement.

Legal restrictions based on age (18+ requirement for claims), jurisdiction, and platform policies will apply. No token distribution is guaranteed.
