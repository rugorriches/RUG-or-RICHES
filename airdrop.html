How the airdrop works

Before any token event, the airdrop runs across six sequential pre-launch cycles, each roughly a 30-day season with its own snapshot. You earn Airdrop Points by playing — and because the player base grows every cycle, the share of total supply rises with each cycle. Later cycles fund a bigger crowd; the earliest players get the largest slice per person and can stack rewards across all six.

How your slice is calculated

  • Per cycle, per player: your share = your Airdrop Points ÷ total points of all qualified players that cycle × the cycle pool.
  • Elite weighting: players who reach Elite (30M points) receive a higher weighting, so deep engagement is rewarded over volume farming.
  • Stacking: cycles are independent — qualify in all six and you earn from all six pools.
  • Integrity: only server-validated points count; flagged or ineligible accounts are removed before each snapshot.

Per-cycle rewards beyond the pool

Every cycle also runs its own leaderboard payouts (top bankers and top inviters), weekly Crew Wars Victory Chests, and quest/achievement rewards — all on top of your snapshot allocation. The earlier cycles distribute the same prize structures across far fewer players.

How to earn points

  • Bank profitable rounds — a slice of every cash-out converts to points.
  • Quests — daily, weekly, monthly and VIP quests pay points on top of $MOON.
  • Daily rewards — streaks, spins, mystery boxes and the lucky draw.
  • Achievements — one-time milestone rewards.
  • Social tasks — follow/join the official channels (5,000 each).
  • Season Pass — +50,000 points plus premium season perks.
  • Referrals & crews — invite friends (you both get 5,000 $MOON) and grow your crew for milestone rewards.

How to qualify

Qualification rewards consistency, not one lucky run. A typical per-cycle target:

✅ Qualified

  • 10,000,000 points in the cycle
  • 20 active days of play
  • 5,000 taps
  • 100 cash-outs

👑 Elite qualified

Reach 30,000,000 points (and meet the qualified bar) for Elite standing and a larger weighted share of the cycle pool.

Thresholds are illustrative and may be tuned per cycle. Higher ranks and VIP improve your standing; flagged or ineligible accounts are excluded from snapshots.

Rewards & payouts

🪂 Cycle snapshot

At the end of each cycle, a snapshot of server-validated points sets each qualified player's share of that cycle's allocation.

🏆 Leaderboard payouts

Recurring leaderboard prizes reward the top bankers and top inviters during the season.

⚔️ Crew Wars chests

Weekly Victory Chests pay $MOON to winning crews, scaling with lead and contribution.

🔁 Points carry

New seasons start fresh for play, while your accumulated standing carries toward your overall allocation.

What happens at launch

After Cycle 6, points are consolidated and — subject to a legal/compliance review — a claim portal opens for any token event, followed by liquidity, listing, and live token utilities (tournaments, marketplace, staking, VIP and governance-lite).
