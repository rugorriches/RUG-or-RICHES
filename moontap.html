❔ Airdrop FAQ
What are Airdrop Points?
Points are your share of the $MOON token pool when the airdrop happens at season end. The more points you hold, the bigger your allocation. They never expire.
How do I earn points?
Every $MOON you bank (cash-outs), plus daily streaks, daily & social quests, milestones and invites — all add Airdrop Points automatically.
How do points become $MOON tokens?
At season end the token pool is split proportionally across all players by points. Your estimate is shown above and updates as you earn.
When is the airdrop?
At the end of Season 1 — see the live countdown above. New seasons start fresh while your points carry your allocation.
Can I lose my points?
No — banked points are permanent. Only your unbanked position is ever at risk of a rug; once you cash out, the points are yours.
Is $MOON a real token?
Not yet — it's off-chain points in this prototype. A tradable token would only launch after legal review, so treat allocations as in-game until then.

❓ How to Play

RUG OR RICHES — pump it, then sell before it rugs.

🪙
Every tap is a bet
Each click on the coin stakes your bet / click into the current round and pushes the live price up. You can bet in $MOON or Airdrop Points — pick which on the Trade screen (⇄) or in 📊 Dashboard.
📈
Live market
Real-time order flow from other traders moves the chart on its own — green candles when buyers win, red when sellers do. Your taps add buy pressure. 🐋 Whale dumps can crash it without warning.
💰
Position & cashing out
Your unbanked position rides the price in real time. Hit SELL / CASH OUT to bank it as stake + profit. Selling at higher risk pays a bigger bonus (Profit Taker upgrade boosts this).
🪤
Rug risk (heat model)
The 🪤 meter is heat — it builds from how overvalued the price is, how big your position is vs liquidity, sellers taking over, and how fast you pump (greed spikes it). At max heat you get a 🚨 DUMP INCOMING warning — sell in time or eat a hard rug. Outcomes: 😱 Flash crash (fakeout dip, recovers), 🩸 Soft rug (you keep 40%), 💀 Hard rug (lose it all). Rug Insurance, Diamond Vault & VIP all soften it.
🛠️
Upgrades
Spend banked $MOON to boost tap power, energy, combo cap, rug insurance, idle Auto-Trader, vault rescue, and cash-out bonus. Each shows its Now → Next effect.
👑
VIP & bet limits
Your max bet per click is capped by VIP tier (None 1K → Bronze 5K → Silver 25K → Gold 150K → Diamond 1M). VIP also boosts earnings, energy and rug protection.
🏆
Ranks, quests & invites
Lifetime banked $MOON levels your rank (🦐→🦖). Clear daily quests for bonuses, and invite friends — you both get 5,000 $MOON, with milestone rewards as your crew grows.
⚡
Energy & boosts
Each tap costs energy, which refills over time (upgrade regen). Random 🚀 2x Boost windows double your pumps — tap fast while active.
📰
Market events
Watch the badge on the chart: 🔥 FOMO Surge & 📰 Bullish News pump harder, 🐻 Bear Raid dumps, 🐋 Whale Accumulation buys steadily, ⚡ High Volatility swings wildly. Events bias the real order flow for a few seconds.
🏆
Win streak & auto-sell
Consecutive profitable cash-outs build a 🏆 streak worth up to +50% payout — one rug resets it. In 📊 Dashboard you can set an auto-sell target to bank gains hands-free the instant price hits a multiple.
💧
Liquidity & slippage
Bigger bets move the price more (real slippage). As holders grow, liquidity deepens and swings calm down. The chart shows live holders, liquidity and market trend.
⭐
Store
Out of $MOON? The ⭐ Store tops up your balance instantly with Telegram Stars — bigger packs give bonus value.
