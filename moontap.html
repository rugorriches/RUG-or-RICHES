❔ Airdrop FAQ
What are Airdrop Points?
Points are your Season 1 qualification score, separate from spendable $MOON and VIP progress. They cannot be wagered.
How do I qualify?
Reach 10M points, 20 active days, 5K taps, and 100 cash-outs during your first 30-day season. Elite status starts at 30M points.
How do points become $MOON tokens?
At season end the pool is split by points earned — capped by your VIP tier (max share ~0.30%, up to 5% at the top tier). Your live estimate is shown above. Points are earned by playing, never bought.
When is the airdrop?
At the end of Season 1 — see the live countdown above. New seasons start fresh while your points carry your allocation.
Can I lose my points?
No — Airdrop Points are locked season score. Rugs only affect your unbanked $MOON position.
Does buying VIP give me more airdrop?
No — VIP never gives you points, and points can't be bought. VIP raises your earn rate and your allocation cap (the max share you can reach), but you still earn every point by playing. You can also climb VIP tiers for free with VIP points from 🏅 quests & referrals.
Is $MOON a real token?
Not yet — it's off-chain points in this prototype. A tradable token would only launch after legal review, so treat allocations as in-game until then.

❓ How to Play

RUG OR RICHES — pump it, then sell before it rugs.

🪙
Every tap is a bet
Each click on the coin stakes your $MOON bet / click into the current round and pushes the live price up. Airdrop Points are locked Season 1 score, not betting fuel.
📈
Live-style market
A simulated market of buyers and sellers moves the chart on its own — green candles when buyers win, red when sellers do. Your taps add buy pressure. 🐋 Whale dumps can crash it without warning. (A shared real-time chart driven by all live players is coming in a later phase.)
💰
Position & cashing out
Your unbanked position rides the price in real time. Hit SELL / CASH OUT to bank it as stake + profit. Selling at higher risk pays a bigger bonus (Profit Taker upgrade boosts this).
🪤
Rug risk (heat model)
The 🪤 meter is heat — it builds from how overvalued the price is, how big your position is vs liquidity, sellers taking over, and how fast you pump (greed spikes it). At max heat you get a 🚨 DUMP INCOMING warning — sell in time or eat a hard rug. Outcomes: 😱 Flash crash (fakeout dip, recovers), 🩸 Soft rug (you keep 40%), 💀 Hard rug (lose it all). Rug Insurance, Diamond Vault & VIP all soften it.
🛠️
Upgrades
Spend banked $MOON to boost tap power, energy, combo cap, rug insurance, idle Auto-Trader, vault rescue, and cash-out bonus. Each shows its Now → Next effect.
👑
VIP tiers & your airdrop cap
18 VIP tiers (Shallows → Trench III). VIP raises your max bet (None 1× → Trench III 38×), earnings, energy, rug protection, and your airdrop allocation cap (~0.30% → up to 5%). Airdrop points are always earned by playing — VIP only lifts your ceiling, never sells points. Climb tiers with VIP points from referrals & 🏅 VIP-point quests, or unlock instantly with TON.
🏆
Ranks, quests & invites
Lifetime banked $MOON levels your rank (🦐→🦖). Clear daily, weekly & 🏅 VIP-point quests for bonuses. Invite friends and you get 10,000 $MOON + 5,000 airdrop pts + 100 VIP points per friend (25,000 / 10,000 / 250 if they're Telegram Premium), plus milestone rewards as your crew grows.
⚡
Energy & boosts
Each tap costs energy, which refills over time (upgrade regen). Random 🚀 2x Boost windows double your pumps — tap fast while active.
📰
Market events
Watch the badge on the chart: 🔥 FOMO Surge & 📰 Bullish News pump harder, 🐻 Bear Raid dumps, 🐋 Whale Accumulation buys steadily, ⚡ High Volatility swings wildly. Events bias the real order flow for a few seconds.
🏆
Win streak & auto-sell
Consecutive profitable cash-outs build a 🏆 streak worth up to +50% payout — one rug resets it. In 📊 Dashboard you can set an auto-sell target to bank gains hands-free the instant price hits a multiple.
💧
Liquidity & slippage
Bigger bets move the price more (real slippage). As holders grow, liquidity deepens and swings calm down. The chart shows live holders, liquidity and market trend.
⭐
Store
Out of $MOON? The ⭐ Store tops up your balance instantly with Telegram Stars — bigger packs give bonus value.
🪂
Airdrop points (Season 1 score)
You earn airdrop points by playing — 8% of every profitable bank, plus quests & social tasks. They're your Season 1 score, not betting fuel and never for sale. To qualify you need sustained play: 10M points, 20 active days, 5,000 taps & 100 cash-outs. Your VIP tier sets the maximum share you can be allocated (~0.30% → up to 5%).
⚔️
Duels, Ranked & Daily
In Ranks → ⚔️ Duels: the 🌐 Duel Board lets you post or accept 1v1 duels (same chart, higher bank wins the whole pot) with live global chat; ⚔️ My Duels tracks your record & rounds; 📊 Ranked is an Elo ladder with monthly seasons and automatic $MOON prizes. Under Ranks → 🐋 Live, the 🏆 Daily Challenge pays the top 10 biggest banks automatically every day.
