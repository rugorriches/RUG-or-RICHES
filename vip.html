What VIP does

VIP is an optional membership that supercharges how you play. Every level stacks real, permanent in-game perks — and the headline perk is a big multiplier on how fast you earn Season Points from gameplay.

🪂 How VIP helps the airdrop, plainly: VIP does two things — it multiplies the Season Points you earn by playing (banking, quests, dailies — up to 36× at the top), and it raises your airdrop allocation cap: the maximum share of a cycle's pool your account can be allocated rises with your tier — from about 0.83% with no VIP up to 5% at Trench III (roughly Shallows ~1%, Reef ~2%, Twilight ~3%, Midnight ~4%, Abyss ~4.7%, Trench ~5%). You never buy points; you still earn every point by playing — VIP only lifts the ceiling. Points & airdrops carry no guaranteed value.
